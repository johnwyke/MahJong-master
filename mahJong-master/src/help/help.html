Game Operation

Menu

The top menu provides user control and preferences settings over the game. In addition to staring a new game and resetting a current game, you can undo moves, toggle sound effects, and load previous games.

Game board

The Mah Jong gameboard consist of tiles arranged according to the rules of Maj Jong. Tiles are played by clicking on them.

Winning

Games are won by clearing the board of all tiles. When a game is finished, you may start a new game, play that game over again, or quit the program.
