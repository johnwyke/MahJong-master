Game Rules

Overview

The goal of the game is to clear the board of all tiles. This is done by selecting pairs of matching tiles. Tiles must be along a vertical edge to be in play.

The Tiles

  • Bamboo Tiles -- range from 1 to 10
  • Circle Tiles -- range from 1 to 10
  • Character Tiles -- range from 1 to 10 and 'c', 'f', 'n', 's', 'w', 'e'
  • Flower Tiles
  • Season Tiles
  • White Dragon Tile

Winning

The game is won when all tiles are cleared from the board
